What is Trello?

Trello is a project management tool. Simple Kanban boards anyone can pick up in minutes. Great for lightweight projects and personal organization.

  • Dead-simple Kanban boards
  • Very generous free plan
  • Power-Ups to extend features

Who it's best for

Trello is a good fit for individuals and small teams who want simple Kanban boards.
