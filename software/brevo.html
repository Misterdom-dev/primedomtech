What is Brevo?

Brevo is a email marketing tool. Formerly Sendinblue. Email plus SMS and automation, priced by emails sent rather than contacts — friendly to big lists.

  • Priced by emails, not contacts
  • Email + SMS in one place
  • Free plan with daily send limit

Who it's best for

Brevo is a good fit for teams with large contact lists who want email + SMS.

Pricing

Brevo offers a free plan to start, with paid upgrades as you grow. See the official site for current plans and pricing. Current offer: Free plan available.
