How to choose

Choose ActiveCampaign if you want businesses that need advanced automation and segmentation.

Choose Mailchimp if you want small businesses sending their first newsletters and campaigns.

Both offer a way to try before you commit, so the safest move is to test your top pick with your own workflow before paying.
