How to choose

Choose Pipedrive if you want small sales teams that want a no-fuss visual pipeline.

Choose Salesforce if you want larger teams that need deep customization and scale.

Both offer a way to try before you commit, so the safest move is to test your top pick with your own workflow before paying.
